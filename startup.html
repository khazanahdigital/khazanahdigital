Why I Started MyWork

"Whoever does not have this fire becomes nothing." -Rumi

At 22, I dropped out not because I hated business - I dropped out to build in the real world - where broken systems snuffed the fire out of 4 million migrant workers now.

When others saw migrant paperwork as an "unsolvable mess," I saw a billion-ringgit opportunity to prove Malaysia could lead. When advisors warned, "Governments won’t change," I asked: "What if we build infrastructure so powerful, they can’t ignore it?"

MyWork is that bet. Not a feature. Not an app. An ecosystem. An ecosystem to build bullets before a war.

I saw the fire in me, and I realised I can help more kindle theirs. This is the playbook Silicon Valley fears: We move faster. Build bolder. And we do it here - turning Malaysia into the blueprint for emerging markets.
